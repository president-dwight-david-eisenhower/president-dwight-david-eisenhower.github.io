President Dwight David Eisenhower Ancestor Database

This is a repository of self-contained HTML files containing direct ancestors of President Dwight David Eisenhower.

Disclaimer

Ancestor data was sourced from the website FamilySearch.org on the dates indicated in the tables below and as such is subject to error and change. The data on the Family Search website is frequently updated by volunteers who transcribe old handwritten documents that may be innacurate.

Rationale

The purpose in creating these files is twofold: preservation of data offline and ease and flexibility of searching. There is a search tool on the website mentioned above, but it has limited usefulness. The search tool built into the database files below allow one to quickly find all ancestors with a given title such as King or name such as Eisenhower, or all ancestors from a given city such as London or country such as England. I created this software for my own use but decided to apply it to President Eisenhower's ancestry after discovering that he is my grandmother's 8th cousin and that Anna "The Prophetess" is also his direct ancestor, which may be of interest to his great-granddaughter Laura.

Usage and downloading

The HTML database files in the table below can be opened in a browser and used online simply by clicking on the link in the table. Or, each one can be downloaded to your local computer by right-clicking on a link and selecting the option Save link as... Once downloaded, HTML files can be opened in a browser even when you are offline. Alternatively, all HTML files including this overview page can be downloaded immediately as a zip file by clicking here.

After collecting all of the president's available ancetry back 24 generations, I extended his tree along some bloodlines back to approximately the year 0 AD so as to include King Bran the Blessed (Archdruid) and Anna the Prophetess daughter of Joseph of Arimathea, who are of personal interest to me and perhaps might also be to Laura Eisenhower, the president's granddaughter. Aside from being interesting for their own sake, Bran and Anna can both be traced much further back into history on the source website.

 

It would have required exponentially more time and memory to extend the president's tree much further back than 24 generations if all known ancestors were to be included, so I decided to use a different approach: go back about two millennia and trace the descendants of this notable couple until they overlap with the president's ancestors. It must be noted that going that far back in time may cross into mythology at some point -- King Bran was said to be a giant, for example -- not to mention that records become less reliable. Also, as mentioned in the disclaimer above, ongoing changes made to the source data sometimes result in broken chains.

Features

  • Self-contained ancestry database usable online or offline, requiring only a browser; no installation needed.
  • Permanent digital record of ancestors even if source website becomes unavailable.
  • Efficient and flexible searching by name, place or date; wildcards may be used.
  • Data includes links to ancestors' source records on FamilySearch.org.
  • Data may be exported in CSV format for use with spreadsheet or database software.
  • Data includes relationship chains leading from Dwight David Eisenhower to every ancestor in the database.
